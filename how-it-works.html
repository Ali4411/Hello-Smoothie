How it works

Three simple steps!

1. Choose Your Smoothies

Each week, you'll get our unique menu of smoothie recipes tailored specifically to your package. Whether you're embracing the vibrant flavors of our Classic blends, fueling your day with our Protein Rich options, or savoring the plant-powered goodness of our Vegan creations, we've got you covered..

Pick your top seven smoothies for the week, creating a menu that's as unique as you are. With Hello Smoothie, it's all about you - your favorites, your way!

2. Receive Your Delivery

Exciting news! Your custom smoothie kit is on its way, packed with all the fresh ingredients you need for a week of delicious blends.

Each ingredient is carefully portioned and packed to ensure maximum freshness when it arrives at your doorstep.

Get ready to unpack your kit and dive into a world of smoothie delight â€” fresh, flavorful, nutritious and ready to blend!

3. Blend and Enjoy!

The moment you've been waiting for! When your smoothie kit arrives, unpack your ingredients and follow the easy-peasy instructions included in your kit.

Add the pre-portioned ingredients to your blender, along with any additional liquid or optional add-ins specified in the recipe.

Blend until smooth, pour into your favorite glass, and sip away, enjoying the fresh flavors and wholesome goodness of your homemade smoothie creation!
